Spanish Art & Architecture

Architectural Wonders

Spanish architecture blends Roman engineering, Moorish artistry, Gothic grandeur, and modern surrealism. Highlights include:

  • Sagrada Família: Gaudí’s unfinished masterpiece in Barcelona with organic, dream-like shapes.
  • Alhambra: A Moorish fortress-palace in Granada, famous for its Islamic tilework and fountains.
  • Seville Cathedral: One of the largest Gothic churches, housing the tomb of Christopher Columbus.

Artistic Mastery

Spanish art spans religious works, surrealism, and modernism. Discover treasures in world-class museums:

  • El Prado Museum: Home to Velázquez’s Las Meninas and Goya’s dramatic paintings.
  • Guggenheim Bilbao: A titanium-clad marvel showcasing contemporary art.
  • Picasso Museum: Journey through the genius of Pablo Picasso in Málaga and Barcelona.
