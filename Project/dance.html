Dances

Spain is renowned for its rich tradition of dances, each reflecting the unique culture of its regions. One of the most famous Spanish dances is flamenco, which originated in Andalusia. Flamenco is characterized by its passionate music, expressive movements, and rhythmic footwork. Other popular dances include the sevillanas, sardana, and jota, each with its own distinct style and history.
