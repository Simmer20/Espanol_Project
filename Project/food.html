Delicious Spanish Foods

Explore the rich flavors of Spain!

Paella

A famous rice dish from Valencia made with saffron, seafood, chicken, or vegetables.

Tapas

Small appetizer-sized portions of different Spanish dishes, often shared with friends.

Gazpacho

A refreshing cold soup made of tomatoes, peppers, cucumbers, garlic, and olive oil.

Tortilla Española

Spanish omelette made with potatoes and eggs—simple, filling, and beloved across Spain.

Jamón Ibérico

Cured Iberian ham, sliced thin and served with bread or cheese—a gourmet delicacy.

Churros con Chocolate

Fried dough pastries served with thick hot chocolate—perfect for dessert or breakfast.
