Regional Languages of Spain 🗣️

Catalan 🏛️

Spoken in Catalonia, Valencia, and the Balearic Islands

Basque (Euskera) 🏔️

Ancient language isolate spoken in the Basque Country

Galician 🌊

Romance language spoken in Galicia, northwestern Spain

Valencian 🏖️

Variant of Catalan spoken in the Valencia region

Andalusian Spanish 💃

Distinctive dialect from southern Spain, home of flamenco
