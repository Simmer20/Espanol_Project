Language & Dialects

Spanish (Castilian) is the official language, but Spain is home to many regional dialects:

  • Catalan (Catalonia)
  • Basque (Euskadi)
  • Galician (Galicia)
  • Valencian (Valencia)
  • Andalusian (South)
